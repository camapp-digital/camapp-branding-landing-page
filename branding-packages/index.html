Ask About My Brand
What is included in a branding package?

Each package includes a different level of logo, color, typography, brand guide, stationery, social media, and mockup support.

How long does branding take?

Timing depends on the selected package and final scope. CamApp Digital confirms the timeline before the project begins.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes. You can start with a smaller package and add more brand assets later when your business needs them.

Do I receive editable files?

Editable source files are included in the Complete Brand Identity Package or can be added depending on scope.

Can you redesign my existing brand?

Yes. Share your current logo, colors, and materials so we can plan a brand refresh direction.

What information do you need?

We need your business name, industry, goals, audience, preferred style, existing assets, timeline, and budget range.
