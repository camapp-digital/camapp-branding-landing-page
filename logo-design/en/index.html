Ask About My Logo
How long does logo design take?

Basic and Standard target 2–3 business days for first delivery; Premium targets 4–5 business days. Timing starts after the completed brief and project confirmation.

How many logo concepts will I receive?

Basic includes 3 concepts, Standard includes 6, and Premium includes unlimited concepts within the confirmed project scope.

What counts as one revision?

One revision is one organized round of feedback applied to the selected design direction. A completely new direction may count as a new concept.

What files will I receive?

Basic includes PNG and JPEG. Standard adds AI and PDF. Premium also includes EPS and SVG.

Can I use the logo for printing and signage?

Yes. Standard and Premium include vector or print-ready formats recommended for large printing and signage.

Can you redesign my existing logo?

Yes. Select “Logo redesign” in the inquiry form and upload your current logo as a reference.

Can I request specific colors?

Yes. Tell us your preferred colors and any colors to avoid in the brief.

What information do you need before starting?

We need your business name, description, audience, preferred style, colors, logo text, intended uses, and any useful references.

How does payment work?

CamApp Digital will confirm the package, scope, payment terms, and start date with you before design begins.

Do I own the final approved logo?

Ownership terms are confirmed with your project. Final approved logo rights are transferred after the agreed payment is completed.

What is the difference between PNG, JPEG, PDF, AI, EPS, and SVG?

PNG supports transparency, JPEG is convenient for general images, PDF is useful for print, and AI, EPS, and SVG are scalable vector formats.

What happens after I select a package?

The selected package appears in the inquiry form. Complete the brief, submit it, and CamApp Digital will review your request and contact you.
